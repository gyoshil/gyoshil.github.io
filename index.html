Putting control of technology
in the educators' hands.

Gyoshil provides the tools educators need to create their own custom apps. Techonolgy provides new ways to learn, but educators are forced to change their teaching to fit the tools. We provide flexible frameworks, so that educators can instead change the tools to fit their teaching.

Sharing educational content across borders.

By commiting ourselves to an ideal of beautiful code, we are creating more than just a product - we are creating an ecosystem. The apps we create have been meticulously designed to allow anyone to create their own educational tool. Since all the content our users produce is released under an open-source license, educators can learn from one another. A English language teacher in Nonsan can use and modify content from a History teacher in Boise, Illinois.
